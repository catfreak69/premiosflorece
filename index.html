About

History of Premios Florece.

  • 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Premios Florece was born in 2020 as an initiative of Jean Manzano, who, at the time, was working as director of a medical cannabis quality control laboratory. With the intention to celebrate the most prominent brands in the industry in a transparent way, allowing patients themselves to vote for their favorite products and companies. Manzano, together with a team of workers and "influencers" from the industry, carried out the first edition virtually.

  • 2021

    First fisical event

    The second edition was held in person and the event was well received by the public.

  • 2022

    Third Edition

    An event of greater magnitude for its third edition in 2022. The brand of Premios Florece was registered as a non-profit organization, reaffirming its commitment to the island's patient community.

  • April 2022

    Vision for the future

    The team believes that through the annual celebration of these awards a healthy competition will be established in the industry, having the voice of the patients as a priority. To inevitably benefit the public and those who work daily with medical cannabis in Puerto Rico.

Live Event

Be part of our 2022 Live Event.

The Premios Florece non-profit foundation presents its third edition of the Florece Awards on Friday, April 22, at the Caguas Botanical Garden. Join us to celebrate the achievements of the industry in Puerto Rico. In a day full of activities for everyone's pleasure. From Health Fair, exhibition, certifications and free renewals by order of arrival, awards, artisans and to finish with a flourish The Earth Day Concert with music by RaiNao and Vivanativa.
